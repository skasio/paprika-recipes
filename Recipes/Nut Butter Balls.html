Nut Butter Balls

Ingredients

15 soft dates, pitted

2 tbsp coconut oil

2 tbsp peanut butter

50 g / 1/2 cup rolled oats

3 tbsp desiccated coconut

3 tbsp cacao powder

1 pinch sea salt

Directions

Add the ingredients to a food processor and mix until everything comes together into a sticky dough. Roll 20-25 small balls between the palms of your hand. Cover them in chopped hazelnuts or coconut.
