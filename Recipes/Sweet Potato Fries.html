Sweet Potato Fries

Prep Time: 2 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: Serves 1 Source: bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

95g sweet potato, cut into fries

1 tsp rapeseed oil

¼ tsp cayenne pepper

Directions

Heat oven to 200C/180C fan/ gas 6.

Put 95g sweet potato, cut into fries, on a baking tray and mix with 1 tsp rapeseed oil and ¼ tsp cayenne pepper.

Bake in the oven for 20 mins.
