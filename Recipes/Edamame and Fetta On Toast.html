Edamame and Fetta On Toast

Servings: 1

Ingredients

165g edamame, podded

½ lemon

30g cottage cheese

2 slices of bread

1½ tsp olive oil

chives

15g fetta cheese

chilli flakes

Description

Step 1
Place the edamame beans in a saucepan over a medium heat and squeeze in the lemon juice. Heat the beans for 5 minutes and use a fork to crush around half of them.

Step 2
Spread the cottage cheese on the toast and top with the edamame beans. Drizzle over the oil then sprinkle over the chives, feta and chilli flakes to serve.
