High Protein Chocolate Chia Pudding

Servings: 2

Ingredients

300g silken tofu

40g cocoa powder

50g peanut butter

30-60g maple syrup (adjust to taste)

125ml protein-fortified soy milk or dairy milk

30g chia seeds

Directions

1. Place tofu, cocoa powder, peanut butter, maple syrup, and milk into a food processor
2. Process until smooth
3. Add chia seeds and stir through
4. Let sit for 5 minutes
5. Stir again
6. Pour pudding equally between two jars
7. Put lids on jars
8. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight to thicken

Notes

Can be topped with 100g Greek yoghurt to increase protein content to 32g per serving.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
