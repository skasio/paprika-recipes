Mini Chicken + Carrot Meatballs

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: Yield: 24 mini meatballs Source: babyfoode.com

Ingredients

500g ground chicken or turkey

1/4 cup applesauce or apple puree

1/2 cup shredded carrot

1/2 cup chopped spinach

1/4 cup chopped green onion

1 egg

1/2 cup breadcrumbs

1/2 tsp cumin

salt

pepper

Directions

1. Preheat the oven
Preheat your oven to 200Â°C. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, a silicone mat, or tin foil.

2. Prepare the mixture
In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients. Gently fold the mixture together using your hands or a spatula until everything is well incorporated. Let the mixture sit for 5 minutes.

3. Form the meatballs
Roll the meat mixture into 2.5 cm (1 inch) balls. Wet your hands occasionally to prevent sticking during the rolling process.

4. Bake
Place the meatballs onto the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until the meatballs are cooked through and gently browned.
