Classic Lentil Tarka Daal (The Spice Tailor)

★★★★★

Store Bought

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Total Time: 25 minutes Difficulty: Easy Servings: 3

Ingredients

500g Classic Lentil Tarka Daal

3 naan bread

225g basmati brown rice

100g yoghurt

160g baby spinach

Directions

Cook rice, daal and naan bread according to instructions and stir baby spinach through the daal.

Serve daal with rice and naan and dollop of yoghurt
