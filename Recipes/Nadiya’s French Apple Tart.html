Nadiya’s French Apple Tart

Source: Nadiya’s Bake me a Story pg.132

Ingredients

175g plain flour

15g icing sugar

125g unsalted butter

1 egg yolk

2 tsp ground cinnamon

8 tbsp apricot jam

50g caster sugar

1 unwaxed lemon

225g red apples

2 tbsp lemon juice

1 tsp caster sugar

900g cooking apples
