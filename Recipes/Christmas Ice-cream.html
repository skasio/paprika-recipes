Christmas Ice-cream

★★★★★

Prep Time: 12 hours Total Time: 12 hours Difficulty: Easy Servings: 20

Ingredients

180g dried raisins

125g dried prunes

180g dried sultanas

180g dried currants

125g mixed glacé fruits

60g chopped mixed peel

1/2 cup orange or lemon juice

60g crushed nut toffee brittle

60g glacé cherries - quartered

60g grated dark chocolate

90g blanched almonds, chopped and toasted

142ml cream (or more)

2 litre vanilla ice-cream

Description

This is a very rich mix and a small serve only is usually needed.

I often just put it into an empty 4 litre ice-cream container.

This is a very hard ice-cream to scoop.

Peanut brittle is usually available from Darrel Lee.

Directions

Chop raisins, prunes and glace fruits and mix with the sultanas, currants and mixed peel.

Sprinkle with juice and allow to stand for several hours.

Lightly and quickly fold all the ingredients into the slightly softened ice cream.

Pack the mixture into a 3 litre mould and freeze overnight.
