Simple Halloumi Stew

Servings: 1 Source: Joe Wicks

Ingredients

1 tsp olive oil

100g halloumi, cut into cubes

1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed

210g tinned chopped tomatoes

80g tinned chickpeas, drained

½ tsp paprika

¼ tsp mixed dried herbs

salt

pepper

Directions

Step 1
Heat the oil in a frying pan. Add the halloumi and fry for a few minutes on each side until golden. Remove the halloumi from the pan and set to one side.

Step 2
In the same pan, fry the garlic for a minute or so then add in the tomatoes, chickpeas, paprika and mixed herbs. Stir everything together and leave to simmer for a few minutes, until thickened a little.
Season to taste.

Step 3
Stir the halloumi back through and serve up.
