Mushroom Biryani with Cucumber Raita

Gousto

Total Time: 10 min Servings: 2 or 4 servings Source: gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

Flat white mushrooms (150g)

Mango chutney (20g) †

Cooked white long grain rice (280g)

Curry powder (1tbsp)

Vegetable stock mix (11g)

Natural yoghurt (80g) †

Roasted garlic paste (15g)

Crispy onions (15g) †

Tomato paste (16g)

Cucumber (0.5pcs)

Chestnut mushrooms (160g)

Sultanas (30g)

Directions

Instructions for 2 [for 3] [for 4] portion recipe

1. Before you begin...

Get all your ingredients and equipment ready

Skim through these steps, then wash your fruit and veg

Squeeze your pouch[es] of cooked white long grain rice to separate the grains

Tear the top corner of the pouch[es] (just a little!) and microwave for 2 min or until piping hot

Tip: If you're cooking two or more pouches, pop them in together but increase the microwave cook time accordingly

3. Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium-high heat

Meanwhile, boil half a kettle

Once hot, tear your flat white mushrooms into large pieces, add them to the pan and cook for 2 min

4. While the flat white mushrooms are cooking, combine your vegetable stock mix, tomato paste, mango chutney and sultanas with 200ml [260ml] [350ml] boiled water – this is your tomato stock

5. Crumble your chestnut mushrooms into the pan and add your curry powder and roasted garlic paste

Cook for 1 min or until fragrant

6. Add the cooked rice to the pan with the tomato stock and increase the heat to high

Give everything a good mix up and cook for 2 min or until the mushrooms have softened – this is your mushroom biryani

7. Meanwhile, grate your cucumber and squeeze any excess water out of the grated cucumber

Combine the grated cucumber with your natural yoghurt and a pinch of salt and pepper in a bowl – this is your cucumber raita

Serve the mushroom biryani with a dollop of the cucumber raita, garnish with your crispy onions and dig in

Enjoy!

Notes

Note: Equipment and pan sizes vary. Make sure you cook food thoroughly (even if it takes you a little longer than 10 min)
